THE GIMLET HUB


Ah Gin, a classic spirit, paramount to the most refreshing elixirs. Why not stir one up while working on your CSS? Our featured Gin cocktail is the GimletHub.

INGREDIENTS

60 mililitres Gin
30 mililitres fresh lime juice
30 mililitres simple syrup (sugar dissolved in water, if you don't have any handy and must make it yourself)

GARNISH - Cucumber slice or Lime wedge


DIRECTIONS

Fill a cocktail shaker (or a smaller glass wedged into a larger one if you are without) with ice and pour in the gin, lime juice and simple syrup. Stir (or shake, for you Mr. Bond) vigorously with a long cocktail spoon (or a regular one, for those of you using a glass not a shaker...) until very cold.
Strain the cocktail into a chilled martini glass or over ice in a glass if you prefer it on the rocks. Garnish with that lime wedge or cucumber slice you put aside. Don't forget to use a coaster; we don't want any condensation leaking into your keyboard!
